Products ordered over time
Charts the amount of one or more products has been ordered over time. This allows you to see which products are most popular, and when they are ordered most often.
Single product ordered over time: Organization comparison
This chart is useful if you belong to several organizations which correspond to locations, campuses, branch offices, etc. It charts the ordering history of one product against one or more locations, so you can determine which locations are most active.
Single product ordered over time: User comparison
Similar to the organization comparison, this charts the ordering history of one product against one or more users, allowing you to gauge demand but also catch any erratic ordering behaviour.
